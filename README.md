# HR Analytics Dashboard | Microsoft Excel

## Overview

This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. The dashboard transforms raw HR data into meaningful insights using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers and KPI Cards.

## Objectives

* Analyze employee attrition across departments and job roles.
* Monitor workforce demographics and salary distribution.
* Identify factors contributing to employee turnover.
* Build an interactive dashboard for HR reporting and decision-making.

## Dashboard KPIs

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Active Employees
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Age
* Average Salary
* Job Satisfaction Rating

## Dashboard Visualizations

* Attrition by Department
* Attrition by Job Role
* Attrition by Gender
* Attrition by Age Group
* Attrition by Salary Slab
* Attrition by Years at Company
* Overtime vs Attrition

## Interactive Filters

* Department
* Business Travel
* Education Field
* Job Role
* Gender

## Key Insights

* Research & Development recorded the highest employee attrition.
* Employees aged 26–35 years showed the highest attrition.
* The 0–5K salary slab had the largest number of employees leaving the company.
* Employees working overtime experienced higher attrition.
* Most attrition occurred during the early years of employment.
